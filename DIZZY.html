Medicine Guide For Dizzy

BONAMINE

Description

Meclizine Hydrochloride (Bonamine®) Chewable Tablet is used for the prevention and treatment of nausea, vomiting, or dizziness associated with motion sickness.

Guide

For 25mg, adults and Children ≥ 11 yrs. old 1 to 2 tablets or, as prescribed by a physician. If additional dose is needed, interval should be 4 hours or longer.

For 12.5mg, children 5-11 yrs old should be limited to two single doses only.
A daily dose should be taken one hour before travel starts for 24-hr protection. The tablet may be chewed and swallowed with or without water or swallowed whole with water.

VICKS VAPORUB

Description

You love to watch your kids sleep and see them lost in their world of dreams. But with a cough & cold, their sleep gets disturbed and their dreams are left incomplete. Use Camphor + Menthol + Eucalyptus Oil (Vicks VapoRub), providing relief from nasal congestion due to cold to help them sleep better!

Guide

For cough suppression, rub a thick layer of Vicks VapoRub on your chest and throat. Cover with a warm, dry cloth, if desired. Keep clothing loose about your throat and chest to help the vapors to reach the nose and mouth. For muscle and joint aches and pains, apply Vicks VapoRub onto the affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily.

KATINKO

Description

KATINKO® Ointment contains Methyl Salicylate, Menthol and Camphor. METHYL SALICYLATE topical (for the skin) is used for temporary relief of muscle or joint pain caused by strains, sprains, arthritis, bruising, or backaches.

Guide

Apply generously to the affected area as often as required or repeat the application 3 to 4 times daily until relieved. Use this medication as directed by your doctor or follow all directions on the product package. If you have any questions, talk to your doctor or pharmacist.
